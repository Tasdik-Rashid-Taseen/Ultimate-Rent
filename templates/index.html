Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of adding my property?

By adding your property to the Ultimate Rent website, you'll be able to rent or sell property online with the help of one of the largest property marketplaces in Bangladesh. Promote your property to thousands of verified property seekers and let Ultimate Rent shoulder the responsibility of finding the right buyer/tenant.

Do I need to sign any agreements?

No, you don't need to sign an agreement to add your property to the website to rent or sell your flat, land, or commercial property. However, you can sign an exclusive agreement with Ultimate Rent to take advantage of their extensive marketing features and facilities.

What is the process of selling/renting my property?

Once you have submitted your request to add a property to the Ultimate Rent website, one of our representatives will contact you for information. After that, an executive will visit your site, take necessary photos/videos, and publish your property on the website to attract buyers. You can also sign an agreement with Ultimate Rent to take advantage of their extensive marketing features and facilities.

How long will it take to find buyers/tenants?

As soon as your property is listed on the website, you'll be able to reach thousands of potential buyers and tenants for your property. And if you decide to sign an exclusive agreement with Ultimate Rent, your property will be directly marketed to property seekers whose requirements match your property.

Are there any charges?

There are no charges to add property on our platform. However, a commission may apply based on the agreement signed between you and Ultimate Rent in the event of a sale.
