Seller Guide: Simplifying Your Property Sale with Ultimate Rent

01

Initial Consultation

Engage in a thorough discussion with Ultimate Rent to outline your selling requirements. Tackle important questions such as the optimal selling price, legal considerations, and strategies to connect with the ideal buyers.

02

Gathering and Validating Property Details

Trust us to craft a tailored plan for you. We collect essential information about your property—location, size, and condition—to evaluate and determine the best selling approach that aligns with your goals.

03

Legal Document Verification

We gather all necessary legal documents to verify property and ownership authenticity, advising on any required updates or missing documents. This step ensures a smooth transaction process by preempting legal obstacles.

04

Professional Property Photography

Our team captures high-quality photographs and videos of your property. This modern viewing method enhances the appeal of your property to potential buyers, making it a more attractive investment.

05

Finalizing Agreements

Before listing, we’ll discuss and agree on service charges and finalize our collaboration with a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) signed by both parties, ensuring your property is represented at its best.

06

Hassle-Free Selling Process

With the preliminary steps complete, relax while we handle everything until your property is sold. We engage with interested buyers—screening their background and financial capacity—before proceeding to property viewings.

07

Drafting Legal Agreements

Upon finding a suitable buyer, Ultimate Rent drafts essential agreements: a Tripartite Agreement among the seller, Ultimate Rent, and the buyer, and a Deed of Agreement outlining payment schedules and property handover details.

08

Property Handover

Following the fulfillment of all conditions, the final step is the handover of the property to its new owner. Congratulations on successfully selling your property through Ultimate Rent!
